Jakob Cansler

Jakob Cansler is currently a senior at Woodside High School in Newport News, Virginia, where he has starred in numerous productions, including Little Shop of Horrors (Seymour), The Dining Room, The Laramie Project, and most recently The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee (Douglas Panch). He has also performed in many community theatre productions, including The Diary of Anne Frank, Sleeping Beauty, and A Christmas Carol. In the past, he has starred in Peter Pan (Slightly Soiled) at Russell Sage College and in The Music Man at Shaker High School.

Currently, Jakob studies as part of the Magnet School for the Arts at Woodside High School for Drama.
